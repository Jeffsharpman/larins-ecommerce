Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products, orders, shipping, and more.

Orders & Shipping

How do I place an order?
Browse our products, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You'll need to create an account or sign in to complete your purchase.
How long does shipping take?
Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days within the US. Express shipping (1-2 days) and international shipping options are also available.
Can I track my order?
Yes! You'll receive a tracking number via email once your order ships. You can also track your order status in your account dashboard.
What if my order is delayed?
We aim to ship all orders within 1-2 business days. If your order is delayed, you'll receive an email notification with an updated delivery date.

Returns & Exchanges

What is your return policy?
We offer a 30-day return policy on unused items in their original packaging. Returns are free for US customers; international returns may incur shipping costs.
How do I return an item?
Contact our customer service team to initiate a return. We'll provide a return label and instructions. Once received, refunds are processed within 3-5 business days.
Can I exchange an item?
Yes, exchanges are available within 30 days. Contact us to arrange an exchange for a different size, color, or product.
What items cannot be returned?
Items that have been used, damaged, or are part of our clearance sale cannot be returned. Gift cards and certain personal care items are also non-returnable.

Products & Ingredients

Are your products cruelty-free?
Yes! All Larins products are 100% cruelty-free. We never test on animals and work only with cruelty-free certified suppliers.
Do you use natural ingredients?
We prioritize natural and organic ingredients whenever possible. Each product lists all ingredients clearly, and we avoid harmful chemicals like parabens and phthalates.
Are your products vegan?
Most of our products are vegan. We clearly label vegan products, and you can filter by this preference in our product catalog.
How should I store my products?
Store products in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Some products may require refrigeration - check the packaging for specific storage instructions.

Account & Support

How do I create an account?
Click "Sign Up" in the header and fill out the registration form. You'll need a valid email address to create your account.
How do I reset my password?
Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and enter your email address. We'll send you a reset link within a few minutes.
How do I contact customer service?
You can reach us via email at support@larins.com, phone at +1 (234) 567-8900, or through our contact form. We respond within 24 hours.
Do you offer gift wrapping?
Yes! Gift wrapping is available at checkout for a small fee. You can also add a personalized message to your order.
